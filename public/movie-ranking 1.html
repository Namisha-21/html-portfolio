The Best Movies According to Namisha

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

A prison drama that makes you feel deeply for all of its characters even without the thrilling end, it would have been a fun watch for everyone.

Forest Gump

A very evenful time in America's history, seen through the eyes of one man, Forest Gump, a man who has a very low IQ, a score of just 75.

Titanic

This movie brings a tear to your eyes. If you want to cry and melt down to a glamurous love story, I strongly recommend that you watch this movie. Titanic is well worth seeing.
